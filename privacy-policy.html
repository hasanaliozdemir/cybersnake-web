Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CyberSnake. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account and Gameplay Information

  • Username and profile information
  • Game progress, scores and achievements
  • In-app purchase receipts (when applicable)

2.2 Device and Technical Information

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (for analytics and advertising)
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data

2.3 Analytics and Advertising Data

  • Ad interactions and impressions
  • Usage patterns and aggregated behavior (anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to operate and improve the game, provide personalized features, analyze usage, serve ads (where permitted), and communicate with you about updates and support.

4. Third-Party Services

We may share data with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including analytics, advertising, push notifications, and hosting. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and deliver advertising. You can control cookie settings via your browser.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose data to service providers, legal authorities when required, or in connection with business transfers.

7. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary to provide the service and for legitimate business purposes, including compliance with legal obligations. Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However no system is 100% secure; we encourage careful handling of sensitive information.

9. Children

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. International Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your residence. Such transfers comply with applicable law.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

12. Contact

Email: privacy@cybersnake.app

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted with a revised effective date.
